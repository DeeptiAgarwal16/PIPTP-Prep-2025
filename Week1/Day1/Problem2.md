## Problem

**Consider the pseudocode mentioned below. For how many times, the while loop will be executed?**

```pseudo
Integer a
  Set a=1
  while (a<5)
    a=a+2
  end while
  Print a
```

---

## Options

- A. 1  
- B. 4 
- C. 2  
- D. 3 

---

## Approach

- a=1, loop will execute and a becomes 3
- a=3, loop will execute and a becomes 5
- a=5, loop will not execute.

## Correct Answer: **2**
